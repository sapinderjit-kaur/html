volunteer opportunities

Thank you for your interest in volunteering at Sai Dham Food Bank! We have rewarding opportunities that make a real difference. From front-line support to unique roles, there's something for everyone.

  • Our vision is to create a hunger-free community where no one goes to bed on an empty stomach and our mission is to provide nutritious food to those in need while fostering a sense of belonging.
  • Volunteers play a vital role in realizing this vision, as they selflessly dedicate their time and effort to ensure that our programs and services reach as many people as possible.
  • With your support, we can make a lasting impact and bring hope to those facing food insecurity. Join us on this mission and be a part of the change.

    FAQS

    What do you serve to the needy?
    We serve non-perishable as well as perishable food items (with a diversity of vegan, kosher meals, non-vegetarian and other cultural preferences) directly to people’s door-step.
    How do I get started as a volunteer?

    To get started as a volunteer, you will need to complete the volunteer registration form and attend an orientation.

    When and where do orientations take place?

    Orientations are typically hosted bi-weekly on Saturday’s. They take place at our warehouse located at 31 Steinway Blvd, Etobicoke, ON M9W 6N3

    What should I bring on my first day?

    We recommend you bring gloves, mask, water and a comfortable pair of shoes (closed-toe shoes recommended) for each shift.

    What are the hours of operation?

    Warehouse hours of operation are between 11:00 AM- 5:00 PM seven days a week. Operations may begin earlier or end later depending on new initiatives.

    Is there a minimum commitment contract/ hours?

    There is no minimum commitment. As a volunteer, you are welcome to contribute as many hours as is feasible for you.

    How do I get proof of hours contributed?

    Volunteers are required to sign in at the beginning of each shift. Hours are logged by the management team. A letter will be provided upon request. If you require documents to be completed from your institution, please bring it with you and a leader will complete it.

    Is Sai Dham Food Bank an essential entity as registered by the Government?

    Due to the nature of our mandate, Sai Dham Food Bank is deemed essential by the Ontario government. We observe all public health guidelines to maintain a safe environment for our team.

    What is the minimum age to volunteer?

    We do not have a minimum age requirement for volunteers. Volunteers under the age of 18 will require express written consent from a parent or guardian in order to contribute.

    Who do I need to contact to request an accommodation?

    If you require an accommodation, please discuss it with your Orientation Lead on your first day.

    How can I get friends/family to join?

    All volunteers are required to complete the volunteer registration form . Once complete they will attend a scheduled orientation to get started.

    Do you accommodate co-ops and/or practicums? How often do you entertain them and how to get in-touch with the team?

    Volunteer hours for co-op or practicums are available for both semesters – September to December and January to April. Please email a resume to Info@SaiDhamFoodBank.com with your name, contact information, program/institution. An interview will be conducted in order to place candidates.
